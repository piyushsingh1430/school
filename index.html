About Our School

Gyan Nikitan Public School is dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning. We offer a vibrant, inclusive environment where every child is encouraged to excel in academics, sports, arts, and leadership.

  • Highly qualified and supportive teachers
  • Modern classrooms and science labs
  • Smart technology and digital learning
  • Focus on values, discipline, and creativity
  • Extensive sports and library facilities
  • Regular field trips, seminars, and cultural events

Our Courses

Primary (Class I - V)
